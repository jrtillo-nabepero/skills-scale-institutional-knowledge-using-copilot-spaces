# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers research user needs, design intuitive interfaces, and validate usability through prototyping and testing. They ensure products are accessible, delightful, and aligned with user workflows and business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and visual designs
- Define interaction patterns and information architecture
- Collaborate with Product and Engineering on feasibility and trade-offs
- Maintain design systems and component libraries
- Validate designs through user feedback and analytics

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences
- Reduce friction and improve user satisfaction metrics
- Ensure consistency across product surfaces
- Advocate for user needs throughout the product lifecycle

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques with cross-functional teams
- User research readouts and persona definitions
- Prototype demos and usability test reports
- Collaboration in planning sessions to clarify acceptance criteria and user flows

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create clear, accurate, and user-centered documentation that helps users, developers, and stakeholders understand and use products effectively. They bridge technical complexity and user comprehension.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user guides, API documentation, and release notes
- Collaborate with Engineering to document features and integrations
- Ensure documentation accuracy through review and testing
- Establish and maintain documentation standards and style guides
- Improve discoverability through information architecture and search optimization
- Gather feedback from users and support teams to refine content

### Goals
- Enable users and developers to self-serve answers efficiently
- Reduce support burden through comprehensive, accessible documentation
- Maintain documentation accuracy and consistency
- Improve time-to-value for new users and adopters

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with Engineering and Product teams
- Collaboration during feature planning to understand user needs and use cases
- Content audits and gap analyses shared with Product and Engineering leads
- Release notes and changelog updates coordinated with Release Manager

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads define and execute testing strategies to ensure product quality, reliability, and adherence to acceptance criteria. They coordinate quality assurance activities across the team and advocate for robust testing practices.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategies and coverage for features and releases
- Create and maintain test plans, test cases, and QA checklists
- Execute manual testing for exploratory, edge case, and acceptance validation
- Coordinate automated testing efforts with Engineering
- Identify quality risks and gaps early in the development cycle
- Report defects clearly with reproduction steps and severity assessment
- Facilitate bug triage and regression testing

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality, reliable software with minimal production defects
- Ensure features meet acceptance criteria and user expectations
- Improve test coverage and automation over time
- Reduce cycle time from development to validated release

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews during sprint planning
- Daily standup participation for status on testing progress and blockers
- Bug triage meetings with Engineering and Product teams
- QA signoff reports before releases
- Retrospective input on quality metrics and process improvements

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain infrastructure, automation, and deployment pipelines that enable fast, safe, and repeatable software delivery. They ensure system reliability, observability, and scalability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation workflows
- Manage infrastructure provisioning, configuration, and monitoring
- Implement deployment strategies (blue-green, canary, rolling releases)
- Establish observability through logging, metrics, and alerting
- Collaborate with Engineering on build optimization and deployment readiness
- Respond to incidents and facilitate root cause analysis
- Ensure security best practices in pipelines and infrastructure

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, and repeatable software deployments
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Reduce manual toil through automation and self-service tooling
- Improve incident detection and recovery times

### Typical Communication
- Deployment planning and coordination with Release Manager
- Incident response and post-mortems with Engineering and support
- Infrastructure capacity planning with Engineering leads
- Pipeline and tooling demos for developer experience improvements
- Collaboration during planning to identify infrastructure needs and constraints

---

## Customer Success/Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Success/Support Leads advocate for customer needs, manage escalations, and provide feedback to Product and Engineering teams. They ensure customers achieve value from the product and drive continuous improvement through customer insights.

### Responsibilities
- Manage customer onboarding, training, and support escalations
- Triage and resolve customer issues in collaboration with Engineering
- Collect and synthesize customer feedback for Product prioritization
- Identify patterns in support requests to highlight product gaps or usability issues
- Develop knowledge base articles and customer-facing documentation
- Coordinate with Technical Writers to improve self-service resources
- Track customer satisfaction metrics and advocate for improvements

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and product adoption
- Reduce time to resolution for customer issues
- Minimize recurring support requests through product and documentation improvements
- Provide actionable customer insights to inform product roadmap

### Typical Communication
- Weekly syncs with Product and Engineering on escalations and trends
- Customer issue triage meetings with Engineering and QA
- Feedback sessions sharing customer pain points and feature requests
- Collaboration with Technical Writers to improve documentation based on support patterns
- Release readiness reviews to understand customer impact and communication needs
